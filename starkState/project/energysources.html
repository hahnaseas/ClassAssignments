Renewable Sources

Why Care?







Energy is crucial for all life. The sun is a source of available energy. Our choices affect the Earth's system in ways that many of us aren't aware of. By spreading awareness of clean energy, we can produce the energy needed without the usage of greenhouse emissions or other harmful substances.



Wind energy is the process which uses wind to generate power to create electricity. The turbines convert kinetic energy into mechanical energy.


Solar power is when the sun shines on solar panels, and the energy is absorbed by the cells within a panel. The panels convert energy from the sun into electricity.


But Wait, There's More!

While wind and solar are the most popular renewable energy sources, there are more sources of clean energy that you may not know including hydropower, biomass, geothermal, and ocean energy!
