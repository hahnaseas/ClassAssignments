Flexbox Thoughts:


Flexbox enables you to arrange and align content within a container seamlessly, even when the complete dimensions are unknown. This inherent flexibility eliminates concerns about overflow, providing a visually cohesive layout. What distinguishes Flexbox from alternatives like CSS and Bootstrap is its one-dimensional nature, offering precise control over item alignment. It prioritizes content alignment over overall site layout. While particularly suited for simpler projects, Flexbox can also accommodate more intricate designs with the addition of supplementary CSS. That being said, I'm interested to see how this method works over these next couple of weeks.
