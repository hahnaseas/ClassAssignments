Meet the Villains

The villains that try to bring about havoc and destroy Kim Possible!



  • Dr Drakken

    Drakken is a mad scientist who dreams of world domination and is Kim Possible's main arch nemesis's. He has access to a lot of [evil] technology, many of which include but isn't limited to machines, robots, potions, and laser weapons.

  • Shego

    Dr Drakken's sidekick and another one of Possible's nemesis. Her weapon of choice is her bright-green (presumably) plasma hands and can also manipulate energy.

  • Duff Killigan

    A proud pro-golfer and turned to crime once he was banned. Calls himself the "World's Deadliest Golfer" and uses exploding golf balls as his weapon of choice.
