Our Warranty:
Throughout every stage of our lives, our stuffed animals undergo change as well. To matted, discolored, and stained plush bears, you name it.

Hug Harbor only wishes the best for its customers. We will send you a new bear if you show us proof of: your receipt, photos of defect(s), and any additional information you feel is necessary.

Warranty is valid for 10 years. We have the right to refuse a product if we don't deem it to be defective.
